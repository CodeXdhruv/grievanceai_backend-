GRIEVANCE DOCUMENT - BATCH TEST 1
===================================

1. WATER SUPPLY COMPLAINT
The water supply in Sector 15, Block C has been irregular for the past 2 weeks. Residents are facing severe hardship as water comes only for 30 minutes in the morning. The municipal department has not responded to our complaints. We request immediate restoration of normal water supply schedule.

2. ROAD DAMAGE ISSUE
The main road connecting Sector 15 to the highway has developed multiple potholes after recent rains. Several accidents have occurred due to poor visibility of these potholes at night. Street lights are also not functioning properly. We demand urgent road repairs.

3. GARBAGE COLLECTION PROBLEM
Garbage collection has stopped in our locality for the past 10 days. The waste is accumulating on streets causing health hazards. Stray animals are spreading garbage everywhere. Please resume regular garbage collection service.

4. ELECTRICITY BILLING ERROR
I have been overcharged in my electricity bill for the month of November. My meter reading shows 450 units but the bill shows 850 units. Despite visiting the electricity office twice, the issue has not been resolved. Please correct the billing error.

5. PARK MAINTENANCE REQUEST
The community park in Sector 15 has been neglected. Playground equipment is broken and unsafe for children. Benches are damaged and the walking track is overgrown with weeds. Request maintenance of park facilities.
