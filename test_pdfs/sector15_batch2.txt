GRIEVANCE COLLECTION - SECTOR 15
Municipal Corporation Chandigarh
Area: Sector 15
Date: December 2024
---

GRIEVANCE 1:
The street lights on main road of Sector 15 near market have not been working for last 2 weeks. People feel unsafe walking at night. We have complained many times but still no response from authorities.

GRIEVANCE 2:
Water supply in Sector 15 is available only for 3 hours in morning. This is not sufficient for families. We need at least 6 hours of water supply daily.

GRIEVANCE 3:
The park near Sector 15 community center has become a dumping ground for construction debris. Children cannot play there. Swings are broken and never repaired.

GRIEVANCE 4:
Road near Sector 15 market towards bus stand is in very bad condition with many potholes. Accidents happening daily. Please repair immediately.

GRIEVANCE 5:
Stray dogs in Sector 15 have increased significantly. They are attacking morning walkers and children. Recently a child was bitten. Animal control should take action.
