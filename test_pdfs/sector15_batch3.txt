GRIEVANCE COLLECTION - SECTOR 15
Municipal Corporation Chandigarh
Area: Sector 15
Date: December 2024
---

GRIEVANCE 1:
Streetlights of Sector 15 main road near the market area are not working since 2 weeks. This creates dangerous situation for people at night. No action despite multiple complaints.

GRIEVANCE 2:
Traffic signals at Sector 15 main crossing are malfunctioning. The red light stays on for too long causing traffic jams during peak hours. Police not helping.

GRIEVANCE 3:
Potholes on Sector 15 market to bus stand road are getting worse every day. Many bikes have fallen. Need urgent road repair.

GRIEVANCE 4:
Water logging near Sector 15 park area has become a mosquito breeding ground. Many residents have fallen sick with dengue. Immediate drainage work required.

GRIEVANCE 5:
Construction work on new building in Sector 15 is happening at night causing noise pollution. Residents cannot sleep. Construction should stop after 8 PM.
