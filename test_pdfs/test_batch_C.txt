GRIEVANCE COLLECTION - BATCH C
Municipal Corporation Ward 8
Date: December 2024
---

GRIEVANCE C1:
The open drain near Shanti Nagar school is overflowing. Students have to cross this area daily and many have fallen sick due to contaminated water splashing on them. This is a health emergency.

GRIEVANCE C2:
Traffic signals at the main crossroad near bus stand are not functioning properly. The yellow light is always on causing confusion and accidents. Police are not regulating traffic either.

GRIEVANCE C3:
Construction of new flyover has blocked our shop entrances completely. No customer can reach us for the past 2 months. We are facing severe business losses.

GRIEVANCE C4:
Stray dogs in Industrial Area have increased significantly. They are attacking morning walkers and children. Recently a child was bitten and hospitalized. Animal control should take action.

GRIEVANCE C5:
The street lights on MG Road from sector 5 to sector 7 are not working since past three weeks. At night it becomes very dark and unsafe for everyone. Please fix them immediately.
